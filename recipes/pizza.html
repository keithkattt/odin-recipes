Easy Homemade Pizza Dough

Description

A homemade pizza crust recipe that's great for beginners. This recipe is easy to learn and throw together on. Here you'll learn how to make the best pizza crust of your life with just a few ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup warm water (110 degrees F/45 Degree C)
  • 1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
  • Bread Flour
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
